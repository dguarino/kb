This tool has been developed having in mind a fruitful interaction between modelers and experimenters.
To improve your chances to get an answer and contribute to the Knowledge-Base, here are some tips:

Do your homework

Have you thoroughly searched for a statement before asking your question?

Be specific

If you ask a vague question, you’ll get no answer. But if you give details, a useful statement will follow.

Make it relevant to the Knowledge-Base

Make it clear how your question is relevant to more people than just you, and more of experts will be interested in your question and willing to look into it.

Be on-topic

Statements are defined by a specific set of topics. Please try to stick to those topics.

Keep an open mind
